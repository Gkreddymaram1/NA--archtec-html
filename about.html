Halit Hajbakri (RIBA,RTPI,MBA)

Associate Architect

Halit Hajbakri is an award-winning RIBA chartered architect and urban planner with over 12 years of experience in the United Arab Emirates, the Gulf Cooperation Council and India, working on residential, mixed-use and transport projects. He holds a Masters in Urban Planning from the University of Birmingham, Dubai, and a Masters in Business Administration from SVU.

Halit's interdisciplinary experience in architecture, engineering and urban planning gives him the confidence to provide exceptional service to his clients. He has worked in various positions including as a Lead Design Architect at Design Centre in Dubai, a Senior Design Architect at Golden Square in Dubai, and an Architect at Mazaya in Dubai. He is currently serving as an Associate Architect at NA Architects in Dubai, where he leads design projects.

Halit is a RIBA Chartered Architect and is certified as an Autodesk Revit Architecture Professional. He is also a member of UAESOE and holds a Dubai Municipality & Tarakhees Licensed Architect (Unlimited). Additionally, he has participated in German-(Arab/Iranian) Dialogue DAAD-(Islamic-European) cities 2010.

Apart from his professional achievements, Halit is fluent in English, Arabic and German, and is an accomplished tennis player and jogger. He holds a UAE driving license.

N. Niroop Kumar Reddy

Founder and Principal Architect

Mr. Niroop Reddy is the Founder and the Managing Partner of NA Architects. With a profound belief that architecture is an extension of our natural surroundings. Niroop Reddy embarked on a mission to create ground breaking and innovative designs that harmonize with the environment. Aside from overseeing the financial health of the company and securing a continuous flow of projects, he is primarily involved in the visioning, planning, and pre-development phase of projects. He is a handson leader, eagerly jumping into sketch, critique, brainstorm, review, and revise ideas for most of the projects in the office. His endless flexibility and excellent prioritization skills are vital as he switches from project to project, all at different phases and posing different challenges. He also leads project presentations to the client and works with his teams to balance ideas for developments with prefeasibility studies and financial models, establishing designs that will maximize profit and best utilize existing conditions.

Niroop has garnered the respect of his team by insisting that they do their homework and produce their best designs in every stage of the project and in every aspect. He is adamant that this level of attention and care is how work should always be done. His design standards promote consistent growth of his team and create exceptional solutions for the client. Through his deep understanding of the client’s perspective, he instructs his team to put themselves into the shoes of the client, listen to their objectives, and relay information with the “whole picture” in mind. Acting as the architect, developer, financier, and cost estimator, depending on what is required, is fundamental in building mutual trust with a client

Driven by a passion for exploring new forms, materials, and styles. Niroop Reddy represents a new generation of architects who constantly push the boundaries of creativity. Sustainability lies at the core of every project under taken by NA Architects, Reflecting Niroop Reddy’s commitment to environmentally responsible design. Niroop Reddy holds the distinction of being an IGBC accredited Professional in India and a registered Green Building Consultant in Dubai, highlighting his expertise and dedication to green practices.

NA Architects is built on the philosophy that great design is thinking in an unconventional way, one that is not limited by existing context and will stop at nothing to deliver the maximum development value. When you link individual components of a master plan accordingly, it transforms into a way of life. The world is no longer siloed; there is a constant blend of culture, people, habits, and differences. NA Architects stands proudly among the top 30 architectural firms in India, according to Forbes, and enjoys unparalleled respect and renown in the world of Architecture.

N. Rupana Reddy

Founder and Principal Designer

Mrs. Rupana Reddy is a Principal and Head of Interior Design who believes that there is no room for an overarching theme in her work, no one “design approach.” Applying one design approach uniformly to all projects ensures that the final design falls short of what could be. She believes that each project takes on its own unique identity and style and must be treated as the distinctive entity that it is. Her love of design stems directly from this belief. Listening in a different way allows her to translate a client’s vision into something that they can see in front of them and experience for themselves.

Rupana has seen a shift in the responsibility of designers over the years. In creating a retail space, designers are now crafting a complex, multi-faceted experience rather than just a “retail store”. They are creating new destinations where people will come together, connect, and interact with one another as well as with their environment. One of Rupana’s greatest strengths is reimagining these spaces, addressing how people will come through the space and what will attract them. As she looks at a project from many different perspectives, she sees the whole picture and designs at a level above expectations for any given space.

She has been felicitated with numerous prestigious awards for her astounding creations which surprise the clientele every time. What’s more, she inspires the younger generation to pursue their dreams in the field of Interior Design and ‘achieve beyond’.

K. Vinod Krishna

Associate Architect

Vinod Krishna Kambhampati is an accomplished architect with extensive experience in designing and coordinating large-scale healthcare projects in India and the UK. His expertise in architectural design, documentation, and coordination has been recognized and appreciated by clients, stakeholders, and colleagues alike.

Vinod's portfolio includes his role as Principal Architect for the Service and Teaching Hospital Facility at Christian Medical College in Vellore, where he was involved in redesigning and developing the hospital's expansion plan. Additionally, he was appointed as the Architect for the extension plan for the existing hospital in Adoor, which included additional OPD, Diagnostics, and ICU with 100 beds IP in its 3.75-acre campus.

Vinod has worked on a variety of projects as an In-House Architect, including the refurbishment of a 500-bed hospital in Chennai to meet Joint Commission International Accreditation processes. He has also worked on international projects, such as the Ray Phillip’s team in preparing detail design co-ordinated drawings of Science Centre during a secondment programme to Bahrain and lead a team in preparing 200 co-ordinated drawings of Mess buildings and handled architectural inputs to multi-discipline co-ordination meetings in OMAN office for all the 61 buildings.

Vinod's experience also includes working on design proposals for apartment projects, a 50-bed clinic, and a beach house for a famous person in his earlier career. Additionally, he has worked as an intern under the guidance of Mr. PS Govinda Rao - a renowned architect who has worked on cineplexes, office buildings, and hospital projects between 1960-2005.

Vinod's skill set extends beyond architectural design to project management, contract documentation, and approval processes. He has also supervised construction activity and co-ordinated design teams to achieve scheduled timelines.

With his expertise and passion for creating healing environments for patients, Vinod is a valuable asset to any project.

M. A Faheem Ahmed

Associate Architect

Mohd Abdul Faheem Ahmed is a senior architect with over 20 years of experience in designing commercial, public, residential buildings, gated communities, and schools. He has a strong track record of creating highly appealing and emotionally engaging designs, which are well-integrated with the surrounding environment. He has a deep understanding of building codes, technical specifications, sustainable building practices, and space planning.

Mohd Abdul Faheem Ahmed has completed his education with A.I.I.A certification, and he is a member of the Society of Engineers Dubai U.A.E and Tarkhees Accreditation Program. He has worked with several renowned architecture firms in Dubai, where he has supervised large teams and delivered complex projects successfully.

In his previous role as a Senior Associate Architect at Ridge Architects Engineering Consultancy Dubai, he supervised a team of over ten members in conceptualizing and designing architectural spaces. At Pioneer Engineering Consultancy Dubai, he was part of an international architectural design firm, serving corporate, education, multifamily residential, and hospitality clients globally. As an Architect at Arabesque Engineering Consultancy Dubai, he supervised a team of three members in designing, served as design lead, and conducted conceptual design space planning, design presentation, and material development.

Mohd Abdul Faheem Ahmed is proficient in AutoCAD, Photoshop, and 3dsMax, which he uses to create dynamic designs independently or in collaboration with associates and clients.

Some of the key projects that Mohd Abdul Faheem Ahmed has worked on include Staff Housing Accommodation for Dubai Civil Aviation and Dubai Air Wings, Ground + 14 for Sheikh Hashar Al Maqhdoom at Al Nahda, Ground + 20 for Mr. Nasir Iqbal at Al Barsha, Gated Community of 22 and 23 villas for PAN Global Real Estates Company at Al Furjan, and Bangladesh secondary high school.

Overall, Mohd Abdul Faheem Ahmed is a talented architect with extensive experience in designing architectural spaces that are aesthetically appealing, functional, and well-integrated with their surroundings. His technical knowledge, creativity, and leadership skills make him a valuable asset to any architecture firm.
